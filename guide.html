Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit: June to September (summer) for outdoor activities, December to February for winter sports.
  • Currency: Bring Kazakhstani Tenge (KZT), but USD and credit cards are also widely accepted.
  • Language: Kazakh and Russian are the main languages. English is limited, so learning a few basic phrases is helpful.
  • Transport: Use Yandex Taxi for convenient and safe rides.
  • Safety: Kazakhstan is generally safe, but be cautious in remote areas.
  • Food: Try local dishes like Beshbarmak, Shashlik, and Kazy.
